About

Meet the founder.

Sebastian Dracic is a 13-year-old software engineer and the sole developer behind Allegro Web. He's been writing code since he was 11 — driven by a genuine obsession with building things that work.

Over three years he's been mentored closely by his father, Damir Dracic — a successful business owner who has built three companies and contributed to developing the NSW health system. That background means Sebastian brings a real business mindset to every project, not just technical skill.

During those three years he's shipped real software at Timeline, IT TESLA, and Laterz Social — building production hardware and software, IoT systems, and social platform features used by real people. The kind of work most developers don't touch until years into a career.

Allegro exists because great websites shouldn't be slow, bloated, or expensive. Every site is built by hand, to a professional standard, by someone who actually cares how it turns out.
